# delete from

<emphasis>delete from</emphasis> 通过条件将数据从表中删除。

<code-block lang="SQL">
    delete from r;
    delete from r where condition;
</code-block>

<note>
    与<emphasis>drop table</emphasis>不同，会保留表结构等。
</note>
